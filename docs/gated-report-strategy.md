## 2. Which Sections Should Be VISIBLE in Free Preview

### ✅ Section 1: Scorecard (100% Visible)
**Why**: This is the core diagnostic result that proves the audit's value. Users need to see their RAG scores to understand the severity of their situation.

**Components**:
- Spend & Visibility RAG rating (Red/Amber/Green)
- Architecture & Leakage Risk RAG rating
- Business Pain & Urgency RAG rating
- Confidence score percentage

**Visual**: Full color-coded cards with animations

---

### ✅ Section 2: Key Insights (Partial - 2 of 3 visible)
**Why**: Show enough value to build trust, but leave one high impact insight for the full report.

**Components**:
- **2 insights fully visible** (the most actionable ones)
- **1 insight blurred** (the most valuable, specific to their business)

**Example**:
```
✓ Insight 1: "You're overpaying for LLM calls due to unoptimized prompt patterns"
✓ Insight 2: "Your staging environment is consuming 40% of your AI budget"
🔒 Insight 3: "Your model routing logic is sending 60% of queries to premium models unnecessarily"
```

---

### ✅ Section 3: Tier Recommendation (100% Visible)
**Why**: This is the diagnostic conclusion that users need to understand their next steps.

**Components**:
- Tier badge (1-4)
- Tier description text
- Priority assessment

---

### ✅ Section 4: Cost Evidence Verification (100% Visible)
**Why**: This builds credibility through data transparency.

**Components**:
- Audit confidence score
- Billed provider
- Monthly spend (self-reported or verified)
- Identified waste summary

---

### ✅ Section 5: Executive Summary (100% Visible)
**Why**: A brief overview that summarizes the key takeaways.

**Components**:
- 2-3 paragraph summary
- Current posture assessment
- AI opportunity overview

---

## 3. Which Sections Should Be BLURRED/LOCKED

### 🔒 Section 6: Detailed Findings (BLURRED)
**Why**: This is where the deep analysis lives. Users who see their specific pain points will be highly motivated to unlock.

**What to blur**:
- All detailed findings with specific numbers
- Specific cost leak identification
- Architecture diagrams and analysis
- Detailed root cause explanations

**Blurred content example**:
```
🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒
```

---

### 🔒 Section 7: Expert Recommendations (BLURRED)
**Why**: These are the actionable steps that users need to implement. Showing them creates immediate value.

**What to blur**:
- Specific implementation steps
- Tool recommendations
- Cost savings calculations
- Timeline estimates

---

### 🔒 Section 8: ROI Calculation (BLURRED)
**Why**: This is the most compelling reason to unlock. Users want to see the financial impact.

**What to blur**:
- Total cost savings over 12 months
- ROI percentage
- Payback period
- Monthly savings breakdown

**Example**:
```
🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒 🔒
```

---

### 🔒 Section 9: Implementation Roadmap (BLURRED)
**Why**: Users want to know the exact steps to take. This creates a sense of direction.

**What to blur**:
- Phase 1, 2, 3 implementation details
- Timeline estimates
- Resource requirements
- Success metrics

## 6. Urgency Strategies (Without Being Misleading)

### ✅ Ethical Urgency Triggers

| Strategy | Example | Why It Works |
|----------|---------|--------------|
| **Time-Sensitive Access** | "Your audit results expire in 24 hours" | Creates FOMO without lying |
| **Limited-Time Bonus** | "Download your report before Friday for a free optimization checklist" | Adds value without deception |
| **Progress-Based** | "You've completed 90% of your audit - finish to see your full report" | Leverages completion bias |
| **Competitive** | "See how your AI costs compare to industry benchmarks" | Triggers comparison instinct |

### ❌ Avoid These (Misleading)
- "Only 3 left in stock" (unless true)
- "23 people viewing this page" (unless tracked)
- "Price increases tomorrow" (unless true)
- Fake countdown timers

## 7. Tier-Based Gating Strategy

### Tier 1 (Strong Fit) - Immediate Action
**Gating approach**: Light gating
- Show more insights visible
- Focus on ROI calculation
- CTA: "Book AI Cost Audit" (direct to booking)

### Tier 2 (Good Fit) - Moderate Gating
**Gating approach**: Balanced
- Show 2 insights visible
- Show blurred ROI
- CTA: "Explore AI Cost Audit" (educational)

### Tier 3 (Future Nurture) - Soft Gating
**Gating approach**: Value-first
- Show all insights visible
- Focus on educational content
- CTA: "Get Cost Optimization Guides" (lead magnet)

### Tier 4 (Exclude) - No Gating
**Gating approach**: Redirect
- Show basic summary
- CTA: "Learn More" (educational content)

## 10. Key Principles

1. **Value First**: Always show enough value to build trust
2. **Curiosity Gap**: Leave just enough out to create desire
3. **Transparency**: Be clear about what's locked and why
4. **Friction Reduction**: Minimize form fields
5. **Trust Building**: Use security badges and social proof
6. **Tiered Approach**: Different gating for different tiers
7. **Ethical Urgency**: Use real, not fabricated, urgency
